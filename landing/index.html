Where to begin

1.Choose your team

A well balanced team is created as a whole. Choose Pokémon that cover each other's weaknesses.

2.Create your team

Your team should be realistic. Think about which Pokémon you already have or could feasibly obtain and breed them.

3.Test your team

Play with your team and fine tune it. After your team is complete, you should test it out and adapt movepools acording to your needs.
